The Out of Office Application

The Idea for the Out of Office application was pitched to me from our mentors at ICAEW, as a way to track employee holidays and time away from the office. I was presented with a high concept problem and we were required to build a solution to solve. The issue was raised due to the fact that managers and employees alike were wasting time in the day trying to find people who may not even be working that day. There was no clear uniform way to quickly track if an employee was absent, training or simply working from home. This led to time being wasted that could have been better spent working on projects that were of a high priority.
